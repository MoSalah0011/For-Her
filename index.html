A Message for My Love

Happy Birthday to the most beautiful soul that has ever walked into my life. Honestly, I have been thinking about how to write this, and no matter how much I try, I still feel like words are not enough to explain how much you mean to me—but today, I will try, because you deserve to hear it all. I found you at the most unexpected period of my life. It was a time when I had no intention of falling in love, but then you came into my world. Slowly, you changed everything. You changed how I understand what love truly means, and you made me realize what a wonderful and beautiful thing love can be when it is shared with someone like you. From the moment you came into my life, everything started changing in ways I never expected. You didn’t just become my girlfriend; you became my peace, my happiness, my comfort zone, and one of the biggest blessings life has given me. Loving you has been one of the easiest and most natural things I have ever done. I love you for who you are—not just for the big things, but for the little things you don’t even notice. I love the way you smile, the way you laugh, the way you care, and even the way you worry about me sometimes. Those small moments mean everything to me. One of the things I admire most about you is how deeply you care for others. You have such a selfless heart that you often care for the people around you more than you care for yourself. You have a special way of making my world feel lighter. On days when life feels stressful or confusing, just talking to you or thinking about you brings calmness to my heart. You make me feel understood, accepted, and genuinely loved, and that is something I will never take for granted. We have been through so much together. I want to say I am sorry for all the things I have made you go through. I know that sometimes I make you mad, I bully you, and I don't always meet your expectations. There are moments when I think to myself that I don’t even deserve to have someone like you in my life. But Insha Allah, I will try as much as I can to be better for her every single day. I admire your strength so much. I admire how kind your heart is, how beautiful your mind is, and how caring you are toward the people around you. You are beautiful in ways that go far beyond what eyes can see. Your personality and your aura make you so special, and I find myself falling in love with you over and over again. Being with you has changed me in so many good ways. You make me want to grow, to be better, and to build something meaningful and lasting. Thank you for loving me, for believing in me, and for supporting me even when things are not perfect. Thank you for choosing me every single day. I know love is not always about perfect moments, but I want you to know that I will always do my best to love you intentionally. I promise to stand by you, to support your dreams, to comfort you when things get tough, and to celebrate your wins like they are my own. You are not just someone I love; you are someone I cherish deeply. You are someone I pray for and someone I am so grateful to share my heart with. If life gave me another chance in another lifetime, I would still choose you again and again without hesitation. You are my happiness. You are my peace. You are my answered prayer. You are my person. No matter where life takes us, I want you to always remember that you are deeply loved.
❤
Yours Always, Mohammad
